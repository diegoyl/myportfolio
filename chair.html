Chair

I wanted to make a chair that can be played with and re-assembled in different ways. There are two identical pieces that can be interlocked to form a chair/stool/surface of varying heights and dimensions. The chair is made of 2x4s, except for the edges which are 4x4s, and was put together with glue.
